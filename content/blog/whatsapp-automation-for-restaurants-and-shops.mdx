The average Indian SME owner spends 2–3 hours a day on WhatsApp. Answering the same questions, sending order confirmations, reminding customers about appointments, following up with leads.

It's necessary work. But almost all of it can be automated.

WhatsApp automation isn't a futuristic concept anymore. Thousands of small businesses across India are already using it — and the ones in Kolkata who adopt it now will have a significant advantage over competitors still doing everything manually.

## What Is WhatsApp Automation?

WhatsApp Business API allows businesses to send automated messages — triggered by specific events or schedules — to customers via WhatsApp.

This is different from the regular WhatsApp Business app (which you use manually). The API allows your systems to send messages automatically based on triggers you define.

Think of it as email automation, but on WhatsApp — where open rates are 95% (vs 20% for email).

## 6 Things You Can Automate Today

### 1. Order Confirmations
When a customer places an order through your website or order system, they automatically receive a WhatsApp message: "Hi [Name], your order #1234 has been confirmed! Estimated delivery: 45 minutes."

No manual typing. No forgetting. Every customer gets instant confirmation.

### 2. Appointment Reminders
Send automatic reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before appointments. A clinic doing this reduced no-shows by 40% within the first month.

The message goes out automatically. You don't think about it.

### 3. Lead Follow-Up Sequences
Someone fills your website contact form. They get an automatic WhatsApp message within 5 minutes: "Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out! We've received your enquiry and will call you within 2 hours. Meanwhile, check our portfolio: [link]"

If they don't respond in 24 hours, a follow-up goes out. Cold leads become warm ones.

### 4. Promotional Campaigns
Festival season? New menu launch? Weekend offer? Send a targeted WhatsApp message to your entire customer list at once — with photos, offers, and a direct link to order or book.

Open rates of 90%+ vs 15–20% for SMS or email.

### 5. Post-Purchase Follow-Up
2 days after a customer visits or places an order: "Hi [Name], how was your experience at [Business]? We'd love to hear your feedback. Leave us a Google review: [link]"

This alone can 3x your Google reviews within a few months.

### 6. Payment and Invoice Reminders
For B2B businesses, freelancers, or anyone with recurring payments — automatic invoice delivery and payment reminders, without the awkward manual follow-up.

## The Honest Limitations

WhatsApp automation has rules (set by Meta):

- You can only message customers who have opted in or messaged you first in the last 24 hours (for marketing messages)
- Message templates need pre-approval from Meta (usually takes 24–48 hours)
- Sending spam will get your number banned

Done properly — with genuine opt-ins and valuable messages — WhatsApp automation is one of the most effective business tools available. Done carelessly, it creates problems.
