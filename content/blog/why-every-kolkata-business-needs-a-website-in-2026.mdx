## The Real Cost of Not Having a Website

Here's a number worth thinking about: **over 85% of customers in India research a business online before making a purchase or visiting in person.**

That means if you don't have a website:

- You're invisible to 85% of potential customers
- You're losing credibility before they even contact you
- You're handing business directly to competitors who do have one

And this isn't a future problem. It's happening right now.

## "But I Have a Facebook Page"

We hear this a lot. And while a Facebook page is better than nothing, it has serious limitations:

1. **You don't own it.** Facebook can change its algorithm, limit your reach, or even suspend your page.
2. **It doesn't rank on Google.** When someone searches "best restaurant in South Kolkata," your Facebook page won't show up — a website will.
3. **It looks unprofessional.** Customers trust businesses with their own domain (`yourbusiness.com`) far more than a Facebook page.

A website is your permanent, owned, Google-ranked home on the internet. Nothing else replaces it.

## What a Good Website Actually Does For You

A well-built website isn't just a digital brochure. Done right, it's a 24/7 salesperson that:

- **Answers customer questions** before they even call you
- **Captures leads** through contact forms and WhatsApp buttons
- **Builds trust** through testimonials, photos, and professional design
- **Ranks on Google** so new customers find you organically
- **Converts visitors into enquiries** with clear calls to action

One of our clients — a restaurant in Kolkata — went from zero online presence to ranking #1 for their local keyword within 60 days of launching their website. Their WhatsApp messages went from 3–4 per day to 20+.

## What Should Your Website Include?

At minimum, your business website needs:

1. **A clear homepage** — what you do, who you serve, why you're the best
2. **Services/Products page** — what you offer and at what price range
3. **About page** — who you are, your story, why customers should trust you
4. **Contact page** — phone, WhatsApp, email, location and a form
5. **Testimonials** — proof that real customers love you

Add SEO to that, and you have a lead-generating machine.
